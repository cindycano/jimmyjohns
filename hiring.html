Hiring

General Manager Area Manager Catering Manager Maintenance Manager

Interview applicants who have been screened by your area manager
 a. The interview guide should be followed to conduct the interview
 b. Always conduct an interview with several purposes
  i. We are to assess the likelihood the applicant will succeed in this position
  ii. We should inform the applicant of what value the position can offer them
  iii. We should set the following expectations
   1. What probationary period is like
   2. What uniform is acceptable (beard length, restrained hair, jewelry, pants, shoes)
   3. How requesting leave works and our leave request policies
   4. What is needed to be successful with us. If you later need to fire this applicant, the task will be much easier if you feel you have given the employee multiple fair opportunities to succeed starting with the interview expectations.
