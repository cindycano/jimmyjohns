Manager Ops Manual

General Manager Area Manager Catering Manager Maintenance Manager

1. Managers must complete ‘Manager Training’ from the Fast Track
a. Completion of the Fast Track qualifies you for certified training
b. Every store should have 2 certified managers

2. Managers are responsible for completing all CMX punchlists on every shift
a. Log into CMX

3. Managers work as a team to improve Audit Completion scores over time:
a. Weekly, remote call meetings are used to discuss strategies to improve scores
b. All managers must read the audits
c. Submit repair tickets to request repair on items that must be corrected

4. Managers must ensure employees abide by the Rules of Employment and managers must also abide by the Additional Rules for Management

5. Managers must ensure that tasks performed by employees comply with both their Fast Track training and The ops manual
a. Always use the mayo scoop to measure
b. Follow all recipes
c. Only serve perfect bread
